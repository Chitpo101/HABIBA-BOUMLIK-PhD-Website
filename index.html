About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Social and Cultural Anthropology from the University of Strasbourg, France. My research focuses on Francophone literatures, North African immigration, and Amazigh identity.

Prior to joining LaGuardia Community College, I held teaching positions across France, Hungary, and Egypt. My pedagogical approach integrates language acquisition with deep historical and social context.
